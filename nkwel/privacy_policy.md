# Privacy Policy

**Protection and respect for your personal data**

Last updated: February 5, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Nkwel, your meal ordering and delivery platform. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and delivery services. Our Nkwel application allows you to order meals from partner restaurants, bakeries, and caterers, track your order in real-time, and have it delivered to the address of your choice.

## 2. Data Collected

### 2.1 Personal Information

- Registration information: last name, first name, email address, phone number
- Addresses: delivery and billing addresses
- Payment information: credit card data (processed securely)
- Dietary preferences: allergies, special diets, culinary preferences

### 2.2 Order Data

- Order and purchase history
- Restaurant and favorite dish preferences
- Reviews and comments
- Geolocation data for delivery

### 2.3 Technical Data

- IP address, device type, operating system
- Application browsing and usage data
- Cookies and similar technologies

### Payment Security

Your payment information is processed by PCI-DSS certified providers. We never store your complete banking data on our servers.

## 3. Use of Data

We use your personal data to:

- **Order processing**: validation, preparation, and delivery of your orders
- **Real-time tracking**: inform you of your order status
- **Customer service**: answer your questions and resolve issues
- **Personalization**: recommendations based on your preferences
- **Communication**: important notifications, promotions (with your consent)
- **Service improvement**: statistical analysis and optimization
- **Security**: fraud prevention and platform protection

## 4. Data Sharing

### 4.1 Restaurant Partners

We share your order information (name, phone number, delivery address, order details) with partner restaurants, bakeries, and caterers for preparation and delivery.

### 4.2 Delivery Personnel

Delivery personnel only receive information necessary for delivery: name, phone number, delivery address, and order details.

### 4.3 Service Providers

- Secure payment processors
- Geolocation and mapping services
- Notification sending platforms
- Hosting and security providers

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for the duration necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

- **Account data**: as long as your account is active
- **Order history**: 3 years for accounting obligations
- **Payment data**: according to regulatory requirements
- **Marketing data**: until you unsubscribe

## 6. Your Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the following rights:

- **Access**: obtain a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification**: correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: delete your data under certain conditions
- **Portability**: retrieve your data in a structured format
- **Objection**: object to processing for legitimate reasons
- **Restriction**: request limitation of processing

### Managing Your Preferences

You can modify your notification preferences, delete your account, or exercise your rights directly from the application in the "Privacy Settings" section.

## 7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- Encryption of sensitive data (SSL/TLS)
- Two-factor authentication available
- Limited data access based on the principle of least privilege
- Continuous security monitoring
- Regular security audits

## 8. Use with Google Play Store

Nkwel is available on Google Play Store. When downloading and using our application via Google Play:

- Google may collect certain data according to its own privacy policy
- Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Google Play Billing
- Reviews and comments are subject to Google Play terms
- We may receive anonymized analytical data from Google Play Console

We encourage you to consult Google Play Store's privacy policy to understand their data collection practices.

## 9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies to:

- Maintain your login session
- Remember your preferences
- Analyze application usage
- Personalize your experience

You can manage your cookie preferences in the application settings.

## 10. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to countries located outside the European Union. In this case, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place (standard contractual clauses, adequacy decision of the European Commission).

## 11. Policy Modifications

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or via a notification in the application. The last update date is indicated at the top of this page.
